A true surreal journey of colors and sound within one green house,
with more than 300 artists together on 8 floors , 24/7 a day
for the past 8 years in the middle of Berlin’s urban warehouse-area,
taking you on a deep dive into the unseen realms of unleashed creativity
never shown to the public before.
Amongst and beyond the transcendent edges
of multi-disciplinary urban and visionary arts,
true hallucinations and authentic contemporary counter-culture,
the feature documentary defuses the mysteries of art by
illustrating the life of the artist;
behind the scenes of the stage of life itself.
The worlds first after-hour movie,
as straight and strictly as Berlins unpredictable underground
- in order to exposure the tone, not tune
of what essentially is the infamous Greenhouse,
broadcasting the love of arts abroad,
beyond „ feiern „ …

Invitation to hosts, lets organise a Screening of Infra:Green and After-Film Discussion
Hello there!

I am thrilled to invite you to host a special screening and after-film discussion of the mesmerizing documentary, Infra:Green. This true surreal journey of colors and sound within one greenhouse, featuring over 300 artists on 8 floors, 24/7 for the past 8 years in Berlin's urban warehouse-area, is a one-of-a-kind exploration of unleashed creativity that has never been shown to the public before.

Infra:Green delves into the transcendent edges of multi-disciplinary urban and visionary arts, unraveling the mysteries of art by illustrating the life of the artist, taking you behind the scenes of the stage of life itself. It's a true depiction of the world's first after-hour movie, capturing the essence of Berlin's unpredictable underground with its authentic contemporary counter-culture, broadcasting the love of arts beyond "feiern" (celebrating).

As someone who shares my passion for art and creativity, I would be honored to have you as my host for this event. I would love for you to join me in expanding on the themes explored in Infra:Green and engaging in a thought-provoking discussion with fellow art enthusiasts.

The screening and after-film discussion will be held on [date] at [time] at [venue/location]. I anticipate a stimulating evening of conversations and insights.

To RSVP or for further inquiries, please reach out to me via WhatsApp at https://wa.me/351929290176. I would be delighted to hear from you and make the necessary arrangements.

Thank you for considering my invitation, and I look forward to your presence at this unique event.
